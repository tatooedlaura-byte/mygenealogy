# Proof Argument: Barbara Slinkard = Barbary Best = wife of Jacob Bess Sr.

**Research question:** Is "Barbara Slinkard" (minor daughter of Jacob Slinkard, Cape Girardeau Co., MO) the same individual as "Barbary Best" (wife of Jacob Best in 1823) and as the wife of "Jacob Bess Sr." (Cape Girardeau / Bollinger Co., MO, 1830–1864)?

**Conclusion:** PROVEN (By Argument). No single record names "Barbara Slinkard, daughter of Jacob Slinkard" as the wife of "Jacob Bess Sr." in one explicit statement, but the combined direct and indirect evidence admits no other reasonable conclusion.

## Known Facts (PROVEN)

1. **Jacob Slinkard (MO) died before Oct 1823.** 1823 Cape Girardeau Co. deed referencing "land confirmed to Jacob Slinkard deceased and owned by him in his life time." (`ev-1823-jacob-slinkard-deceased-deed`)

2. **Barbara Slinkard had a guardian** (Jacob Miller), implying her father was deceased and she was a minor at the time. The distribution names her husband: "Jacob Miller, guardian of Barbara Slinkard… paid her share to her husband Jacob Best." (`ev-jacob-miller-guardianship-barbara`)

3. **Jacob Best and wife Barbary participated in the 1823 deed** disposing of land of "Jacob Slinkard deceased." Barbary's presence as a grantor in her late father's land is consistent with her status as an heir. (`ev-1823-jacob-slinkard-deceased-deed`)

4. **Jacob Bess Sr. resided in Cape Girardeau Co., MO**, in 1822 (`ev-1822-tax-list-jacob-bess`), 1830 (`ev-1830-census-jacob-bess`), and 1850 (`ev-1850-census-jacob-bess`); later in Bollinger Co. (`ev-1859-jacob-bess-deed`, `ev-jacob-bess-probate`). Born ~1798, North Carolina.

5. **Surname spelling.** Jacob Bess Sr.'s aka includes "Jacob Best." Surrounding records use "Best" in 1814–1823 and "Bess" in later Missouri records — a phonetic variation well attested in this cluster.

## Analysis

### A. Barbara Slinkard = Barbary Best (wife of Jacob Best)
The Jacob Miller guardianship distribution explicitly names Barbara Slinkard's husband as Jacob Best. The 1823 deed independently shows Jacob Best and wife Barbary disposing of land that belonged to Jacob Slinkard, deceased — exactly the disposition expected when an heir's land share is being released through her husband. Two independent records, one naming the marriage directly and the other showing the couple acting in the role of Slinkard heirs, converge on the same identification.

**Conclusion:** PROVEN that Barbara Slinkard married Jacob Best.

### B. Jacob Best (1823) = Jacob Bess Sr. (1830–1864)
The man identified as Jacob Best in 1823 is the same individual as Jacob Bess of Cape Girardeau / Bollinger Co. in 1830, 1850, and beyond, based on:

- **Continuity of location.** Cape Girardeau Co. presence in 1822 (tax), 1823 (deed), 1830 (census), 1850 (census), and Bollinger Co. (carved out of Cape Girardeau in 1851) thereafter.
- **Age progression.** 1830 and 1850 census ages place birth ~1790–1800, consistent with an adult Jacob Best marrying ~1814.
- **Household structure.** Children appearing in Jacob Bess's 1850 household (Emanuel, Barbary [daughter], Jacob Jr., Matilda, Daniel) and in the 1868 partition (`ev-jacob-bess-partition-1868`) match a family begun around 1814–1820.
- **Spelling pattern.** Best/Bess phonetic variation is the dominant pattern across the entire cluster (Boston Best Sr. → Peter Best/Bess → Jacob Best/Bess), not an anomaly here.

**Conclusion:** PROVEN that Jacob Best (1823) and Jacob Bess Sr. (1830–1864) are the same man.

### C. Negative evidence
No competing Jacob Best or Jacob Bess appears in Cape Girardeau / Bollinger Co. in the same period to whom Barbara Slinkard's marriage could attach. No record contradicts the identification. The earlier "spouse UNKNOWN" hypothesis (preserved as `REPLACED` per the no-delete rule) was based on Barbara's absence from the 1859 deed and probate — but that absence is fully explained by Barbara predeceasing Jacob, not by her never having existed.

## Conclusion

By chaining (A) and (B):

```
Barbara Slinkard ── (PROVEN, ev-jacob-miller-guardianship-barbara) ──► wife of Jacob Best
Jacob Best       ── (PROVEN, identity argument) ──────────────────────► Jacob Bess Sr.
∴ Barbara Slinkard = wife of Jacob Bess Sr.
```
